Update: I've updated the FC-generation model to use a VAE instead of a GAN.

VAEs are known to have many superior properties compared to GANs.

My current project involves working in the latent space of functional connectivity and genomic information. To this end, I have created a generative model for the latent space, currently accepting phenotype information and generative sample FCs. The model is essentially a WGAN regularized with FC-specific priors. Take a look, play around!
