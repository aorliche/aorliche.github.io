Update Oct 23 2023: I've made some modifications to the evaluation function. The AI is now very strong from an inferior position, and almost unbeatable from ahead.

I've finished a multiplayer/AI implementation of the Russian card game Durak. There are two difficulties: easy (random moves) and medium (heuristics + search).

The search really makes itself known at the end of a two-player game.

You can have up to 4 players in any combination of humans and AI.
