Mon Jun 6 2023 Angle Basis: Generative Model for Functional Connectivity Preprint Hide

Angle basis decomposition for one subject using one basis of 264 sine waves and 264 jitter values.

Almost everyone working with fMRI has used or is at least aware of functional connectivity (FC). We have developed a low-dimensional decomposition and generative model for FC which has some very nice properties:

  1. 10x compression
  2. similar predictive value for reconstruction and residual
  3. 97.3% residual identifiability compared to 62.5% for FC
  4. modest 5% AUC classification accuracy increase for ensemble
  5. data augmentations
  6. ability to generate synthetic FC based on patient characteristics, leading to better intuition and interpretability
  7. no need to have a population to perform decomposition; a single subject is enough

A copy has also been posted on TechRxiv.

Fingerprinting with the angle basis residual clearly identifies the same subject scan pairs, while those pairs are less obvious with FC or Factor Analysis.
