Gang Qu and I recently participated in the IEEEXtreme Programming Competition 16.0. Teams were allowed up to 3 members, but we couldn't find a third. We finished 649 out of 6373 registered teams, although only 3521 actually submitted any code. This puts us in the top 19% of competing participants.

Some of the best team scores were truly impressive, getting more than 90% of all possible points (we got about 25%).
