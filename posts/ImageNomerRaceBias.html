Wed Feb 1 2023 Using ImageNomer to Identify Racial Bias in Functional Connectivity Hide

We've recently posted a preprint on arXiv and Techrxiv describing our development of ImageNomer, a visualization and analysis tool for fMRI and omics data. In the process, we found that all of the intelligence prediction accuracy from functional connectivity data is due to racial confounds. In fact, we find that race prediction (85%) from FC data is slightly better than sex prediction (78%).

The ImageNomer software is available as an alpha version on GitHub, and the preprint is available here.
