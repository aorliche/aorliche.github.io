Latent Similarity

August 30, 2022: Our pre-print has been posted on arXiv!

Latent similarity is a tool used to estimate differences between subject endophenotypes. It makes its estimate based on response variables such as age, sex, disease status, or intelligence scores. We find it is especially robust in the small-sample-size regime.
