What about pigs?

Pigs are known to be avaricious, but are generally pleasant looking. They have also provided important contributions to the field of resource utilization - excepting the sub-discipline of accoustics. And they are great football players, on account of their high fat content.

You may sometimes see a hog in a suit and tie, but if you do, please help them out!
