What about pigs?

Pigs are indeed avaricious, but are generally pleasant looking, and have provided important contributions to the field of resource utilization (excepting the sub-discipline of accoustics). They are great football players, on account of their high fat content. You can often see a hog in a suit and tie, but when you do, please help him or her out!
